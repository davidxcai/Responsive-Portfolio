About Me

Hey there, my name is Dafydd (David). I'm a student web developer trying to create a portfolio website. In my spare time you can find me playing guitar and recording music. Gaming has been a part of me for as far as I can remember and it gets me through a lazy Sunday. As inexperienced as I am, I still deeply enjoy cooking because it fills me with a sense of accomplishment.

Currently I work as an occupational therapy assistant (COTA/L). I work with the elderly and help them rehabilitate/become independent in their everday lives. I chose to become a software developer because I enjoy being creative and it seems like the right amount of fun and stress. I tend to lose my train of thought and change topics at a whim with no indication. I love corgis and pandas and my current favorite color is cerulean blue.
